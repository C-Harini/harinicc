Kuli Paniyaram

These savory,round fritters are made form a batter of fermented rice and urad dal and cokked in a special kuzhi pan.

There are crispy on the outside and soft inside, often flavored with onions, curry leaves and spcies. Kuzhi paniyaram is typically served with coconut chutney or sambar,making it a delicious and unique snack or breakfast item.

Mutta Kola Urundai

A specialty form the Chettinad region,these are spicy,deep-reid meatballs made from minced mutton mixed with a blend of aromatic spices and herbs.

They are crispy on the outside and tender on the inside,often enjoyed as a flavorful appetizer or side dish.

Karaikudi Sambar

The version of sambar comes from the Karaikudi region and is known for its distinct tangy flavor. It features a unique spice blend with freshly ground coriander, cumin,and red chilies,combined with vegetables and tamaring to create a hearty and flavorful lentil stew.

Ragi Mudde

A nutritious and traditional dish made form finger millet flour. Ragi Mudde is a type of soft, round dumpling often served with spicy sambar or curry.It's a staple in many Tamil households and is known for its health benefits and hearty texture.

Chettinad Pepper Chicken

A spicy and aromatic chicken curry from the Chettinad region,known for its robust flavor profile. It features a blend of freshly ground black pepper,red chilies, and a variety of spices,resulting in a fiery yet flavorful dish that is depely rooted in Tamil Nadu's culinary traditions.
